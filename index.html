  1. Ironeye

    Ironeye is a long-range bow attacker with unlimited arrows.
    His skill “Marking” creates enemy weak points, making it easy to break their stance and land critical.
    His passive “Eagle Eye” increases item drops for both himself and allies, helping the whole party collect more loot.

  2. Wylder

    Wylder is a balanced attacker with strong survivability.
    Their passive “Sixth Sense” lets them cheat death once per battle, making them reliable in tough fights.
    Wylder starts with Buckler Parry for early critical strikes.
    The ultimate skill, “Onslaught Stake,” delivers massive damage, breaks enemy stance, and keeps Wylder protected with Vow of the Indomitable.

  3. Executor

    Executor starts with a weapon that causes Blood Loss—great for quickly chipping away boss HP.
    With an S-rank Arcane stat, they boost all status effects, making Blood Loss even stronger.
    Their skill "Cursed Sword" allows well-timed parries to nullify attacks, rewarding skilled players with big advantages in tough fights.

  4. Duchess

    Duchess is a fast, agile attacker who can double-dodge with low stamina use and excels with rapid, multi-hit weapons—especially those that inflict status effects.
    Her skill “Restage” copies recent actions for back-to-back big damage.
    The ultimate, “Finale,” makes the whole party invisible—even while attacking—perfect for surprise attacks or safe revives.

The remaining classes aren’t recommended for solo play, but if you want to challenge yourself, don’t hesitate to give them a try!
